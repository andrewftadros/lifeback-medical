## Spread Health in the Workplace

Whether you own a company, work in HR, or are simply a team player, we want to help your organization prioritize employee health. Working alongside existing corporate wellness programs, we bring healthy snack packs or schedule a healthy food truck to come to your office for the purpose of educating your workforce on using their health insurance benefits for prevention.

We want everyone to take better care of themselves, and this is a fun and casual way to show employees how they already pay for great benefits that are easy to use and hugely valuable. This service is complimentary and is a building block of what we are all about: spreading health.

## Need Help Developing a Corporate Wellness Program?

We are happy to help your organization identify its health goals and implement them. We will meet with you to go over options and costs and design a program that fits your workforce and addresses its most important health-related issues.

Utilizing local vendors and personal relationships helps us offer your team the most convenient solution to getting and staying healthy. We can help you design both short-term challenge or season-based wellness programs and long-term engagement programs. Working with a team of specialists, we add positive energy to your daily workplace environment.

## Customizable Offerings

Here are examples of services we can tailor to your organization:

- **Group Fitness Classes** — Onsite at your offices, locally offsite, or both
- **Personal Training** — Tailored onsite activities that can be done while working or gym-based instruction
- **Registered Dietitian** — Onsite group classes on weekly meal preparation or personalized kitchen visits at employees' homes
- **Family Activities** — Bowling nights, local hikes, and other curated healthy family-friendly outings
- **Urine & Blood Testing** — Easy and sometimes necessary on-site health screenings brought directly to you

## A Partner in Prevention

Our corporate wellness programs go beyond one-time events. We work with your organization to build sustainable health habits that benefit both employees and the company. Healthier employees mean fewer sick days, higher productivity, and a more positive workplace culture. Let us help you invest in the well-being of your team.
