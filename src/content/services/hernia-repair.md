## Hernias Happen

A hernia occurs when an organ or tissue pushes through a weak spot in the surrounding muscle or connective tissue. While hernias are common, they do not resolve on their own. Losing weight, exercising, or using support belts may help with discomfort, but surgery is the only real solution to permanently heal a hernia.

Since hernia surgeries are considered both a medical and a cosmetic issue, one of our general surgeons will team up with one of our plastic surgeons in order to provide you with the most skilled approach to your repair.
