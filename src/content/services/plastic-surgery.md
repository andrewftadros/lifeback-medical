## Personalized Plastic Surgery Services

We offer a variety of plastic surgery services tailored to your individual needs and goals. From excess skin removal to breast reduction and augmentation, our team is here for you every step of the way.

All of our surgeons are board certified and will guide you through the entire process to find the best possible solution for your needs. Whether you are seeking reconstructive procedures following weight loss surgery or cosmetic enhancements to improve your confidence, our experienced surgical team delivers expert care in a supportive environment.

## Our Approach

Every patient receives a thorough consultation to discuss their goals, review medical history, and develop a customized surgical plan. Our surgeons take the time to explain each procedure in detail, including what to expect before, during, and after surgery, so you can make an informed decision with confidence.

We prioritize minimally invasive techniques whenever possible to reduce recovery time, minimize scarring, and deliver natural-looking results. Our interdisciplinary team works closely together, and in cases involving hernia repair or post-bariatric body contouring, our plastic surgeons collaborate with our general surgeons to achieve the best outcomes.

## Common Procedures

Our plastic surgery services include but are not limited to:

- **Excess skin removal** following significant weight loss
- **Breast reduction** to relieve discomfort and improve quality of life
- **Breast augmentation** tailored to your desired outcome
- **Abdominoplasty (tummy tuck)** for a firmer, smoother abdominal profile
- **Panniculectomy** to remove excess skin and tissue from the lower abdomen
- **Body contouring** after bariatric surgery
